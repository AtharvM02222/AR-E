AR ENTERPRISE started in 2019 — Aryaman and his father, a first drone barely staying airborne. Six years later that same obsession has produced custom carbon-fiber FPV racers, Betaflight-tuned freestyle machines, and a dead-cat rig built for cinematic aerial work. Every frame is self-designed. Every stack is hand-soldered. Every drone is flown before it ships. Competed at TRYST IIT Delhi, NYRC and EXUN — the builds have been tested where it actually counts.
